Grandma's Famous Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Taco shells
  • Lettuce, cheese, and tomatoes

Instructions:

1. Cook the beef in a pan. 2. Add taco seasoning. 3. Fill taco shells with beef and toppings. Enjoy!
